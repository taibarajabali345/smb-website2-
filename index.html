SMB Fatima Jinnah Government Girls School

About The School

In 2007, Zindagi Trust embarked on its School Reform Program, with the adoption of SMB Fatima Jinnah Government Girls School as its first school. This marked the beginning of a transformative journey forthe institution. Under the dedicated efforts of Zindagi Trust, SMB Fatima Jinnah underwent significantchanges and witnessed remarkable progress.

To uplift the school's quality, Zindagi Trust implemented various policies and interventions. One such initiative was the inclusion of a Supplementary Syllabus, which enriched the curriculum and provided students with a more comprehensive education. Additionally, School Consolidation efforts were undertaken to streamline resources and improve efficiency.

Through these measures, SMB Fatima Jinnah emerged as a beacon of educational excellence, demonstrating the positive impact of Zindagi Trust's School Reform Program. The success of this initiative serves as an inspiration for other government schools to replicate and enhance their own educational standards.
